items:
  - title: Advanced Programming Languages (Guohao Engineering Elite Class)
    image:
      src: /assets/img/courses/AdvancedProgramming.jpg
      alt: AdvancedProgramming
    description: This course is recognized as a <strong>Shanghai Municipal First-Class Undergraduate Course</strong> and a <strong>Shanghai Key Course (AI+ Course)</strong>. It is also designated as a <strong>Key Course and Smart Course at Tongji University</strong>. The course was awarded the <strong>Second Prize</strong> in the <strong>2025 National University Teaching Innovation Competition</strong>. Offered as an <strong>undergraduate degree course</strong>, it focuses on advanced programming paradigms, language design principles, and high-performance programming practices, tailored for students in the Guohao Engineering Elite Class.

  - title: Data Mining
    image:
      src: /assets/img/courses/DataMining.jpg
      alt: DataMining
    description: This course is a core course of the National <strong>“AI 101” Initiative in China</strong>. Prof. Yufei Chen is a <strong>co-author of the textbook Pattern Recognition and Data Mining</strong>, which is used as a primary reference for the course. The course is designed as an <strong>undergraduate degree course</strong>, covering fundamental theories and methods of data mining.
    
    
  - title: Exploring Medical Artificial Intelligence
    image:
      src: /assets/img/courses/ExploringMAI.jpg
      alt: ExploringMAI
    description: This course is a <strong>premium interdisciplinary course at Tongji University</strong>, designed to introduce fundamental concepts, methodologies, and applications of <strong>medical artificial intelligence</strong>. It focuses on the integration of AI techniques with clinical practice, emphasizing real-world medical scenarios and interdisciplinary innovation.
