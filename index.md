<p>
  <strong>Chen Yufei</strong>, PhD, is an <strong>associate professor</strong> at the School of Computer Science and Technology, <strong>Tongji University</strong>. She received her Bachelor’s degree from East China Normal University and Doctor of Engineering degree from Tongji University, and once served as a Guest Researcher at universities and research institutions in Germany.
</p>

<p>
  She has long focused on the research of machine learning, deep learning and <strong>intelligent medical image analysis</strong>. Her research team has published more than 50 high-level papers, obtained 15 authorized invention patents, and presided over a number of national and ministerial-level projects, winning awards such as the First Prize of Shanghai Technical Invention.
</p>

<p>
  She teaches several core courses, has won teaching competition awards, participated in or taken charge of the construction of key courses, guided students to win awards in scientific and technological innovation competitions, and was awarded the title of "My Ideal Supervisor" of Tongji University in 2024.
</p>
  
<p>
  In addition, she holds positions including Member of the National Technical Committee on Bionics Standardization, positions in several international conferences, and Member of the Specialized Committee of relevant societies.
</p>
